About

My aims are motivated by my proactive style, multitudes of successes, and personal drive. My curiosity about the world and the culture around us inspired me to write, resulting in the publication of two novels, Left Untold and Destiny and Dreams – In Between We. Through literature and dance, I strive to tell stories that inspire, connect, and endure.
