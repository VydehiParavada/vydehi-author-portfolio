About Me

I am an author passionate about human emotions, social realities, identity, and resilience. My writing reflects real-life experiences, struggles, and the emotional strength required to rise after challenges.

I explore topics like gender perspectives, silent emotional battles, dreams vs reality, and how people rebuild life after setbacks.

My stories aim to inspire readers to reflect, heal, and rediscover hope through literature.

Featured Books

Left Untold

The word "girl" still sends negative vibes across mindsets. Why does society still hold gender bias? What if a girl is born with dreams, ambition, and emotional depth? This story follows a determined girl from the womb to adulthood, exploring her struggles, emotions, and aspirations.

Every girl carries an untold story shaped by expectations, silence, and hope. This journey reflects her search for love, freedom, identity, and a meaningful life filled with dreams and emotional truth.

Destiny and Dreams

Destiny and Dreams is about the conflict between what we wish for and what life has planned. Two young men move to a new city chasing ambition, unaware that destiny already has its own path.

The story explores whether dreams shape destiny or destiny reshapes dreams, questioning life, choices, and the unpredictable journey of human ambition.
