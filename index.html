About Me

I am an author, storyteller, and dancer who believes in expressing emotions through words, movement, and imagination. My journey blends literature and performing arts, where every story reflects identity, culture, and human connection.

I write to inspire, to preserve emotions, and to create meaningful narratives that resonate with readers across different walks of life.
